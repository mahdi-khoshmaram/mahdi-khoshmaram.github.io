Neo4j Graph Visualization
This graph which is named EMCKG, illustrates the relational structure of ChatDoctor dataset using Neo4j. The entities in the EMCKG include disease, symptom, drug recommendation, and test recommendation. The relationships in the EMCKG include possible_disease, need_medical_test, need_medication, has_symptom, can_check_disease, possible_cure_disease. Nodes represent entities, and edges denote their relationships, following the approach outlined in This paper published in 2024. This visualization supports the analysis by revealing key interconnections within the dataset.
