About Me

I am currently completing my Master's degree in Computer science at K. N. Toosi University of Technology and I plan to pursue a PhD in the near future. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from University of Tabriz, where my studies significantly strengthened my mathematical abilities and analytical thinking. Both the K. N. My main research in NLP focuses on relation extraction, particularly using Large Language Models (LLMs) and knowledge graphs to enhance information retrieval in medical documents through techniques like Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). Additionally, I've applied LLMs in other innovative projects, such as developing AI-powered glasses that interpret sign language. While NLP is my primary focus, I am also eager to explore other areas of machine learning, broadening my expertise and contributing to a wide range of applications.
