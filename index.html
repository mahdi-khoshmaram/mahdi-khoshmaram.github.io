Publications
Pre-submission
  • Saeed Farzi, Mahdi Khoshmaramzade, Sina Mansoori
    RAG-Enhanced LLMs for Relation Extraction in Medical Documents with Custom Knowledge Graphs
    Our research focuses on zero-shot relation extraction from medical documents utilizing open-source large language models, such as LLaMA 3.1. Furthermore, we enhance these models with a knowledge graph constructed from National Institutes of Health(NIH) databases, leveraging graph databases like Neo4j to optimize performance. Our objective is to improve reasoning in LLMs and reduce hallucination, thereby increasing the accuracy and reliability of the extracted information.
  • Boshra Pishgoo, Mahdi Khoshmaramzade
    Predicting Presidential Election Likelihoods with Social Media Data and Large Language Models
    Our research addresses the absence of a system capable of estimating an individual's likelihood of becoming president based on public opinion. By gathering data from social media platforms such as X, we aim to extract relevant information and assess each candidate's chances. This task is accomplished using open-source large language models (LLMs) enhanced by Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG).
